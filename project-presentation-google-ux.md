## Design Contributions at Google

### 01 — On the Go

**Enhancing Communication: Google Meet, Anywhere**

As lead designer of this feature, I crafted an intuitive audio-only experience that allows users to seamlessly join meetings while walking, driving, or using public transportation. I optimized call controls for single-handed use, ensuring users could safely and effortlessly participate in meetings while on the move — triggered by movement detection. This innovative UI adaptation, with user safety and minimal distraction at its core, resulted in a **granted patent**.

---

### 02 — Family Mode

**Reimagining Video Calls: A Playful Family Experience**

I led the visual design for Google Duo's family-friendly video calling experience, transforming ordinary calls into moments of joy and connection by designing a playful interface with interactive doodle functionality.

---

### 02 — Web Group Call

**Google Duo on the Web: A Consistent Cross-Platform Experience**

This project focused on crafting a comprehensive grid system for Google Duo on the web, ensuring a consistent user experience across various devices. Close collaboration with UX engineers resulted in a flexible and scalable design that adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes while preserving a unified visual language. The outcome is a cohesive and intuitive interface that enhances usability and accessibility for all users.

---

### 03 — Material You

**Integrating the New Google Material Visual Language**

I spearheaded the initiative to bring a more personalized and premium experience to Google Duo and Meet by leading their transition to **Material 3**.

---

### 04 — Foldable

**Meet on Foldable: Adapting to the Future of Mobile Productivity**

This project focused on designing the Google Meet experience for foldable devices, optimizing the interface to adapt seamlessly to different postures and screen configurations. The adaptable design ensures an optimal layout whether the device is folded, unfolded, or in tent mode — maximizing screen real estate and enhancing multitasking capabilities. This project demonstrates the ability to design for emerging technologies and create innovative solutions that adapt to evolving user needs.
