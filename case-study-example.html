BugMap: Issue intake that doesn't overwhelm

Role: Product Designer · 2025

Summary

BugMap helps teams triage user-reported issues without drowning in noise. The goal: make prioritization obvious and fast.

Problem

Incoming issues were scattered across channels; lack of signal caused slow triage and missed high-impact problems.

Outcome

Time-to-triage dropped by 37%. The team agreed on a common language for urgency and ownership.

Process

  1. Mapped sources of bug reports; defined a normalized schema.
  2. Designed board with clear lanes; added filters for churn risk.
  3. Built a minimum lovable version as a Chrome extension.
